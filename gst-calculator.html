About GST

What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. It replaced multiple cascading taxes and unified the Indian market.

GST Rate Structure

Rate Category Examples
0% Exempted Fresh vegetables, milk, bread
5% Essential goods Tea, coffee, edible oil, medicines
12% Standard Butter, cheese, mobiles, processed foods
18% Standard Soaps, toothpaste, capital goods, IT services
28% Luxury & sin goods Cars, ACs, cigarettes, luxury hotels

1 Adding GST

Use "Add GST" when you have the base price and want to calculate the total price including GST. This is useful for pricing products or services.

Example:

Base Price: ₹1,000
GST Rate: 18%
GST Amount: ₹180
Total Price: ₹1,180

2 Removing GST

Use "Remove GST" when you have the total price including GST and want to find the base price and GST amount separately. This helps in accounting and tax filing.

Example:

Total Price: ₹1,180
GST Rate: 18%
Base Price: ₹1,000
GST Amount: ₹180

GST Calculation Formulas

Add GST: Total Amount = Base Amount × (1 + GST Rate/100)

Remove GST: Base Amount = Total Amount / (1 + GST Rate/100)
