Understanding GST

What is GST?

GST stands for Goods and Services Tax. It is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. GST has replaced multiple cascading taxes levied by the central and state governments.

GST Components

GST in India has three components: CGST (Central GST) collected by the central government, SGST (State GST) collected by state governments, and IGST (Integrated GST) collected by the central government for inter-state transactions.

GST Benefits

• Eliminates cascading effect of taxes
• Uniform tax structure across India
• Simplified compliance procedures
• Increased transparency in taxation
• Boost to 'Make in India' initiative
