Weight Converter

Free online weight converter tool that converts between kilograms (kg), grams (g), pounds (lb), ounces (oz), stones (st), and tonnes (t). Features real-time conversion, adjustable precision, conversion history, and export options.

Key Features

  • Convert between kilograms, grams, pounds, ounces, stones, and tonnes
  • Real-time conversion as you type
  • Adjustable decimal precision from 0 to 8 places
  • Conversion history with local storage
  • Export results as CSV or JSON
  • Copy and share conversion results
  • Mobile-responsive design

Common Weight Conversions

  • 1 kilogram = 1000 grams
  • 1 pound = 0.45359237 kilograms
  • 1 ounce = 28.349523125 grams
  • 1 stone = 6.35029318 kilograms
  • 1 tonne = 1000 kilograms
  • 16 ounces = 1 pound
