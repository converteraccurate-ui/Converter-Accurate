About Age Calculation

This calculator determines your exact age based on the dates provided. It accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you a precise calculation.

How Age is Calculated

  • Years: Full years since your birth date
  • Months: Complete months since your last birthday
  • Weeks: Complete weeks since your last birthday
  • Days: Days since your last birthday

Note: If no "Calculate Age As Of" date is provided, today's date is used.
